ABOUT



Telugu Titans is a franchise of the Pro Kabaddi League. The team was founded in 2014 and has participated in the League since it's launch that same year. Season 2 of PKL (2015) saw us fight our way to the play offs and stand second in the League Table. This was also the year that Greenko stepped up to become our primary sponsor, and we decided to retain Rahul Chaudhari, marking the beginning of a long and rewarding relationship with the poster boy of Indian Kabaddi. We made it to the top 5 in Seasons 3 and 4 (2016) as well, but the trophy has eluded us thus far. We are now well into our 6th Season as a Pro Kabaddi team, and look forward to getting our first taste of the championship in 2018.


Background


Hyderabad based Telugu Titans is currently owned by Veera Sports led by Srinivas Sreeramaneni (also Co-founder and chairman of Core Green group) and backed by Mahesh Kolli of Core Green group, Anil Kumar of Greenko group and Gautham Reddy of NED Group.
The team show their spirit of unity coloured in yellow, black and purple with their catch phrase for the year that goes as "Together we are one".


Brief History: Season 1 to 6


In season 1, Founded the franchise and played Season-1 of ProKabaddi League.
In season 2, Advanced to get a position in playoff & stood 2nd in the league table. Signs on Greenko as their sponsor. Retains Rahul Chaudhari.
In season 3, One of the top 5 teams in the ProKabaddi League standing. Retains Rahul Chaudhari.
In season 4, Advanced to get a position in playoff. Retains Rahul Chaudhari.
In season 5, Continues with Greenko as the sponsor. Retains Rahul Chaudhari.
In 2018, season 6, Jagmohan signed on as Head Coach. Linn Caleb Seth joins as Fitness Trainer. Rahul Chaudhari retained.
