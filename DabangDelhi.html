ABOUT



Dabang Delhi KC are also known as 'The Eagles' as the eagle resonates Dabang. The eagle symbolizes perspicacity, courage, strength and immortality, but is also considered "king of the skies" and messenger of the highest Gods. With these attributed qualities the eagle became a symbol of power and strength.
The Dabang Delhi KC logo has 3 attributes to it. The wings symbolize the wings of an eagle; the shield is a symbol of a warrior the gold is desire of always being the #1 team.


Background


Dabang Delhi is owned by Radha Kapoor, under the banner of her own DoIt Sports management company. Although the past seasons were nightmarish for the young owner, she looks to have some hope in this year's revamped squad with Hooda at the helm. The sole female-owner is symbolic of the feminism surge in the country now.
The Eagles, as they are affectionately called by their fans, have the logo bearing an eagle's wings with a golden shield. The logo symbolizes the team's ambition to soar the skies and win wars like the golden warrior who bears the shield.


Brief History: Season 1 to 6


In season 1, Founded the franchise and secured fourth place in the season.The Bengaluru Bulls had a great start to the Pro Kabaddi League 2014 season. They finished third on the table behind U Mumba but advanced to the playoffs.
In season 2, The PKL 2015 saw them give a similar performance and finish third yet again. However, this time they reached the finals after beating Telugu Titans 39-38. Unfortunately, they lost 36-30 to U Mumba in the finals..
In season 3, Bangalore team faced a huge setback in the 2016 season when they finished seventh on the table.
The 2016 season was the worst phase the Bangalore team went through. They lost 12 out of 14 matches and were sitting at the bottom of the table. The starting match was a win. However, their performance declined with each defeat in the league
In season 4, They finished sixthth on the table.They lost 8 out of 14 matches.They are still in the declining stage.
In season 5, The team couldn't make a huge comeback in the next season finishing fourth in Zone B, and not qualifying for the playoffs.
In 2018, season 6,Finally the Bengaluru Bulls Beat The Zone A Toppers, Gujarat Fortune Giants by 38-33 in the final with the Man Of The Tournament Pawan Kumar Sehrawat stealing the show with a record 22 raid points against the young Fortune Giants defence. The coach of Bengaluru Bulls, Randhir Singh was happy as his team finally won the tournament after underwhelming finishes in the previous couple of seasons.
